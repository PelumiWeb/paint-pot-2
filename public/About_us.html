About us..

PAINT POT is a premium brand of decorative wall and floor finish manufactured by AVAN ELYON GLOBAL CONCEPTS. PAINT POT is an indigenous paint manufacturing company that specialise in paint production, colour development, product,colour selection and paint application. Our range of interior and exterior decorative finish include; emulsion,eggshell, satin,textured paint,weather guard, Flex coat,Gloss,floor paint, marble trowel, venetian plaster,brick paint,epoxy,pop glue and many more.
